This system helps organizations move from manual or spreadsheet-based training management to a structured and reliable digital solution.

## Project Overview
The **Training and Courses Management System** is built to support HR and training departments in organizing internal training programs efficiently.

The system provides a single platform to:
- Manage employees and training data
- Control course nominations and attendance
- Track training history and results
- Generate organized reports and documents

The system is flexible and can be customized to match different organizational workflows.

---

## Core Features

### Employee Management
- Create and manage employee profiles
- Store essential employee information
- Track employee participation in training courses

### Course Management
- Create, edit, and schedule training courses
- Define course dates, locations, and duration
- Assign trainers or responsible departments

### Nomination and Selection
- Nominate employees for specific courses
- Control eligibility and approval processes
- Prevent duplicate or invalid registrations

### Attendance Tracking
- Record attendance for each training session
- Maintain accurate training records for employees

### Exams and Results (If Applied)
- Link exams to courses
- Store results and completion status
- Support evaluation and performance tracking

### Reports and Documents
- Generate attendance lists and course summaries
- Prepare printable letters and training documents
- Maintain historical records for reference and auditing

## Future Capabilities

### Advanced Administration
- Admin dashboard with statistics and analytics
- Training performance analysis by department and period

### Notifications
- Automated email notifications for:
  - Course nominations
  - Course confirmations
  - Schedule updates
- Optional SMS integration

### Roles and Permissions
- Admin
- HR / Training Manager
- Trainer
- Employee  
Each role will have controlled access based on responsibilities.

### Certificates and Completion
- Automatic certificate generation after course completion
- Downloadable and printable certificates

### Reporting and Export
- Export reports to PDF and Excel formats
- Advanced filtering by course, employee, department, and date

### Multi-Department Support
- Support for multiple departments or branches
- Centralized management with controlled data access
